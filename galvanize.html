Why Galvanize?

Heroes Never Die!

I have always been a fan of computers, particularly on the hardware side. There is something quite fun in building the perfect gaming rig. It wasn’t until my brother graduated from CU with a degree in Computer Science and got a job at some indie company, Google I believe it is called, that I started looking into it. I am a very rule based individual. I enjoy math, chemistry, schedules, and making spreadsheets. Programming appeals to that side of me. Machines understand things very black and white, 1s and 0s. So I started looking at various bootcamps. It just so happens that I met a graduate from Galvanize, along with finding out that a company my friend works at hires as many graduates as they can get their hands on. The both told me great things about the school so I signed up. After getting the coding challenge, having no knowledge of coding whatsoever, I blazed through the Code Academy course on Javascript and was immediately hooked. I am excited to jump further down the rabbit hole and become a coding pro.

After Galvinize

You're Powered Up, Get In There!

Once I graduate, I would like to work for a small to medium sized company that focuses on social justice. Anything from helping the homeless, veterans, women’s rights, children’s safety, marriage equality, the list goes on and on. Something where I can use my Sociology background along with my new programming skills to make an impact. Cliche I know, but there you have it.
